üë§ Customer Guide

Registration Process

Step 1: Click "Register as Customer" on the homepage
Step 2: Fill in your personal information:
  • First Name and Last Name
  • Email Address (must be unique)
  • Phone Number (must be unique)
  • Password (minimum 8 characters)
  • Address details (optional but recommended)
Step 3: Verify your email address using the OTP sent to your email
Step 4: Complete your profile setup
üí° Pro Tip: After registration, you can login to your dashboard to start booking services immediately!

Booking a Service

Step 1: Browse available services on the "Services" page
Step 2: Select the service you need (e.g., AC Repair, Refrigerator Repair)
Step 3: Click "Book Service" and fill in the booking form:
  • Appliance type and model
  • Detailed issue description
  • Upload images/videos of the problem (optional)
  • Service address
  • Preferred date and time
  • Additional notes
Step 4: Submit your booking and wait for confirmation
üìã Quick Actions:
  • View your dashboard to see all bookings
  • Check booking status in real-time
  • Update your profile information

Managing Your Bookings

Once logged in, you can:

  • View all your bookings in the "My Bookings" section
  • Track booking status in real-time
  • Cancel bookings (before service starts)
  • View technician details and contact information
  • Add notes or additional information

üìã Complete Booking Process

Customer Side

1. Service Selection
Customer browses available services and selects the appropriate repair service.
2. Booking Creation
Customer fills out the booking form with appliance details, issue description, and preferred timing.
3. Confirmation
System generates a unique booking number and sends confirmation to the customer.
4. Status Tracking
Customer can track the booking status in real-time through their dashboard.

Admin Side

1. Booking Review
Admin reviews the new booking and customer details.
2. Technician Assignment
Admin assigns an available technician based on skills and location.
3. Notification
System notifies the assigned technician about the new booking.

Technician Side

1. Booking Acceptance
Technician reviews the booking details and accepts or rejects the job.
2. Service Execution
Technician visits the customer and performs the repair service.
3. Job Completion
Technician updates the job status and provides final pricing.
4. Payment Processing
Customer pays for the service (manual payment system).

üîß Troubleshooting

Common Issues and Solutions

Login Problems

Issue: Cannot login to your account
Solution:
  • Check if your email and password are correct
  • Use the "Forgot Password" feature to reset your password
  • Ensure your account is verified
  • Clear browser cache and cookies

OTP Issues

Issue: Not receiving OTP emails
Solution:
  • Check your spam/junk folder
  • Wait a few minutes for the email to arrive
  • Request a new OTP if the previous one expired
  • Contact support if the issue persists

Booking Issues

Issue: Cannot create a booking
Solution:
  • Ensure all required fields are filled
  • Check if the selected date is in the future
  • Verify your profile information is complete
  • Try refreshing the page and submitting again

File Upload Issues

Issue: Cannot upload images or documents
Solution:
  • Check file size (max 2MB for images, 10MB for videos)
  • Ensure file format is supported (JPG, PNG, PDF, MP4)
  • Check your internet connection
  • Try uploading one file at a time

Browser Compatibility

For the best experience, use one of these supported browsers:

  • Google Chrome (latest version)
  • Mozilla Firefox (latest version)
  • Safari (latest version)
  • Microsoft Edge (latest version)
